Our applications use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services are essential for providing our core functionality and improving user experience. We have carefully selected these partners to ensure they maintain high standards of data protection.

Google Play Services

Used for app distribution, in-app updates, authentication, and core functionality on Android devices. Google Play Services helps us provide a seamless and secure experience across Android platforms.

We use AdMob to display advertisements in our applications. AdMob may use your Advertising ID to show personalized ads based on your interests and browsing behavior. You can opt-out of personalized advertising in your device settings under "Ads" or "Privacy" settings. Non-personalized ads will still be shown to support our free services.

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app, helping us improve user experience and app performance.
