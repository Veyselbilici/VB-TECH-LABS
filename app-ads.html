This file is used to verify our AdMob publisher account and ensure proper ad monetization for our mobile applications. It is a requirement by Google AdMob for transparency in digital advertising.

📋 What is app-ads.txt?

The app-ads.txt file is an IAB Tech Lab initiative that helps ensure transparency in the mobile app advertising ecosystem. It allows app developers to publicly declare the companies authorized to sell their ad inventory, helping to combat fraud and unauthorized inventory sales.
