🎭 1. Application Overview

Zart Zurt is a free entertainment application that provides:

  • Funny cartoon characters and animations
  • Troll sounds and sound effects
  • Joke and prank content for entertainment
  • A fun, lighthearted experience with no competitive or social features

The application is designed purely for entertainment purposes. It does not require any user account, login, or registration to use. All features are available without providing any personal information.

🔒 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely transparent: Zart Zurt does not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • User accounts, passwords, or login credentials
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • Messages, call logs, or browsing history
  • Financial or payment information

🎯 Minimal Data Principle

Since Zart Zurt is a standalone entertainment app with no user accounts or social features, we follow a strict minimal data principle. The app functions entirely on your device without requiring any personal data.

🔧 3. Third-Party Services

Our application may use the following third-party services for functionality and analytics purposes. These services may collect certain non-personal, anonymized data:

Google Play Services

Used for app distribution and core functionality on Android devices. Google Play Services is required for the app to function properly on Android. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Google AdMob

We use AdMob to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) and use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads, including personalized ads based on user interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings. For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Firebase Analytics

We may use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports, helping us improve app performance and user experience. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. For more information, see Firebase Privacy Policy.

📢 4. Advertisements

Zart Zurt is a free application supported by advertisements. We use Google AdMob to display ads within the app. These ads may include:

  • Banner ads displayed at various positions in the app
  • Interstitial ads shown between screens or actions
  • Rewarded ads that users can choose to watch for additional content

Ad networks may use your device's Advertising ID to serve relevant advertisements. You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization

🔐 5. Data Security

Although we do not collect personal data, we are committed to ensuring the security of our application. We implement the following measures:

  • All third-party SDKs used are from reputable, well-known providers (Google)
  • We regularly update our app and dependencies to address security vulnerabilities
  • The app does not request unnecessary device permissions
  • All network communications use encrypted (HTTPS/SSL) connections

👶 6. Children's Privacy

Zart Zurt is a fun entertainment app suitable for a general audience. However, the app is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users. Since no registration or login is required, no personal data of any kind is collected from users of any age.

🎈 Family-Friendly Content

While Zart Zurt contains cartoon characters and fun sounds suitable for general audiences, parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor their children's app usage and manage ad preferences through device settings.
